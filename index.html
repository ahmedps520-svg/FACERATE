About FaceScan AI

FaceScan AI analyzes facial geometry entirely on your device using MediaPipe's on-device face landmark detection. No image, video, or measurement ever leaves your phone — there is no server, no account, and no cloud storage.

Results are app-generated estimates derived from geometric measurements. They are not scientific, medical, or objective assessments of attractiveness.
